We have been here for you for over 30 years

The history of the LS Tractor brand began in 1977 in South Korea thanks to the cooperation of two companies - KHIC and FIAT. This year marks the launch of the production of the first agricultural tractors of the LS Tractor brand and with it a new look and quality. Another milestone is reached by LS Tractor in 1984, when it begins cooperation with well-known brands producing durable engines - MITSUBISHI and YANMAR. Over the years, LS Tractor has grown and gained worldwide recognition. LS Tractor factories were established in Brazil, China and the USA and, together with a branch in South Korea, they created 4 LS TRACTOR production plants in the world.

The popularity of the LS Tractor brand is growing and the best proof is the high level of customer satisfaction. LS tractors have been voted the best in the USA for over 6 years in terms of overall customer satisfaction, availability of tractors and spare parts.

Our company SIP SK sro is a representative of the LS TRACTOR brand in Slovakia. We started our operations in 1992. We deal with sales, service and professional consultancy in the field of professional agricultural, municipal, forestry and garden equipment.
